What It Includes

Five integrated modules

Each module mirrors a deliverable a tech consultant would produce — from raw data through insights.

🔢
Synthetic Data Generator
Generates 24 months of realistic monthly financials — revenue, COGS, headcount, operating expenses, cash balance — using NumPy with controlled noise and baked-in problem signals like growth deceleration and cost surges.
src/data_generator.py
📐
KPI Engine
Computes all six key performance indicators from raw financial data using Pandas: Gross Margin %, Revenue Growth MoM, Operating Expense Ratio, Revenue per Employee, Burn Rate, and Runway.
src/kpi_engine.py
🚦
RAG Benchmarking Layer
Compares each KPI against hardcoded SaaS SMB industry benchmarks (SaaS Capital, OpenView, Bessemer) and assigns Red, Amber, or Green status — the same framework used in real consulting engagements.
src/benchmarks.py
💬
Consultant Findings
Auto-generates 3–5 plain-English insights from RAG results and 6-month trend data, prioritized Red → Amber → Green. Each finding includes a headline, analysis, and a concrete recommendation — mimicking a real advisory deliverable.
src/insights.py
📊
Interactive Streamlit Dashboard
A five-section dark-themed dashboard with company selector, date range slider, tabbed Plotly charts (time-series, bar, waterfall, radar), a color-coded KPI scorecard table, and the auto-generated findings panel.
app.py
💧
Cash Flow & Burn Analysis
A P&L waterfall chart for the latest month (Revenue → COGS → OpEx → Net Income) paired with a dual-axis cash balance / net cash flow trend — the view a CFO or investor would look at first.
Section 05 in app.py
Fictional Profiles

Two companies, two stories

Each company has a distinct financial profile with problem signals baked in — so the dashboard always surfaces meaningful findings.

NovaSaaS
SaaS / Project Management
Founded 2020

B2B project management SaaS with healthy early growth that decelerates over 24 months. Starts with strong 74% gross margin but faces COGS creep and a marketing spend surge at month 14 that compresses the OpEx ratio into the red zone.

Baked-in problem signals
Revenue growth decelerates from ~5.5% → ~2% MoM
COGS ratio drifts +0.25pp/month (infrastructure scaling)
$28K marketing spend surge at month 14
Hiring sprints at months 6, 12, 18
Final RAG (Dec 2024): 1 GREEN 1 AMBER 4 RED
CloudForge
SaaS / DevOps
Founded 2021

DevOps tooling SaaS pursuing aggressive growth with thin margins from day one. Revenue grows faster early on (~7% MoM) but decelerates sharply. High cost-per-head and a large sales surge at month 10 drain cash — runway hits zero by end of period.

Baked-in problem signals
COGS ratio starts at 38% and drifts rapidly higher
$45K sales/marketing surge at month 10
$11K cost-per-head (vs $9.5K for NovaSaaS)
Cash runway reaches 0 by December 2024
Final RAG (Dec 2024): 0 GREEN 0 AMBER 6 RED

What this demonstrates

Each module maps directly to a skill a tech consulting or data engineering role would look for.

🏗️
Data Engineering
Synthetic data generation with controlled noise parameters, seeded randomness for reproducibility, and embedded problem signals designed to surface analytically interesting findings.
📊
Financial Analytics
Translating raw financial line items (revenue, COGS, OpEx, cash) into the 6 KPIs most relevant to SaaS SMB investors and operators — using standard Pandas computation pipelines.
🚦
Business Intelligence
RAG status framework with directional benchmark logic (higher-is-better vs lower-is-better KPIs), scored against real SaaS Capital and OpenView industry thresholds.
💬
Insight Generation
Rule-based narrative engine that reads RAG status, computes 6-month trends, and outputs consultant-style plain-English findings prioritized by severity — no LLM required.
🎨
Data Visualization
Multi-chart Plotly dashboard: area line charts, RAG-colored bar charts, waterfall P&L, dual-axis cash flow, and a normalized radar chart — all with a consistent dark-theme layout.
🧱
Software Architecture
Clean modular project structure: data → kpi_engine → benchmarks → insights → app. Each layer is independently testable. Streamlit caching applied for performance.
